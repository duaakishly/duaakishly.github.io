About Us

We are supportive community at RHU that provides multiservices for all students.We aim to develop students' skills in various aspects of life social, personal and educational

Our Mission

Provide sufficient skills , and to encourage and enable students with the basic knowledge of humanity.

Provide equal opportunities and service to all students through making various educational and motivational workshops, activities and services .

Establish an educational framework/service to help RHU students academically.

Our Vision

We aim to highlight the importance of learning in human life through demanding topics,events, activities and workshops.

This vision will be achieved through assisting students at RHU in their educational, social and personal life.

At the end,we look forward to nurture leaders capable of driving development and contributing to the enrichment of the community.

Our Values
